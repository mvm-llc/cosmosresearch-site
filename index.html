THE CONSTELLATION BUILD

What you're experiencing

  • You've got ceremonies and frameworks in place but they're not helping you make better decisions
  • You're being asked to prove ROI for things you weren't confident about in the first place

Common questions

What does "fractional" mean in practice?
I am embedded with your team for ~10 hours per week — giving you senior product leadership without the cost of a full-time executive hire. I help drive real decisions, while being flexible enough to scale with your needs.
How quickly can we start seeing results?
Most teams will have actionable insights within the first 4–6 weeks. By week 12, you'll have a research-backed roadmap, trained team members, and measurably better product decisions.
Do I need a product team already in place?
Not necessarily. I've worked with solo founders, small teams, and large cross-functional organizations. The engagement model adapts to where you are and where you need to go.
What makes your approach different from a traditional UX consultant?
My super power is working with your existing data — support tickets, analytics, customer feedback — before recommending any new research. This means faster insights, lower costs, and solutions grounded in what your users are already telling you.
What does a strategy call look like?
It's a free 30-minute conversation. We’ll map what’s pulling your product off course and name the few next moves that matter most. You will leave with at least one clear insight and a recommendation on whether a 12 Week Product Reset fits.
